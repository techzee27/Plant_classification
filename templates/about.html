Agronomy with AI

We're building the infrastructure to identify, track, and prevent crop diseases globally before they compromise global food security.

"Crop diseases cost the global economy over $220 billion annually. We believe AI is the key to preventing this catastrophic loss."

Dr. Groot Ai started as a research project to see if modern convolutional neural networks could outperform traditional pathology tests in speed without sacrificing accuracy.

Today, our infrastructure processes thousands of images, providing farmers with instant, lab-grade diagnostic capabilities right from their smartphones. We leverage transfer learning to continuously adapt our models to new pathogens.
