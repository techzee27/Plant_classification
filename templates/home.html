Instant Analysis

Get disease diagnosis in under 2 seconds. Our GPU-accelerated model processes your image instantly, leveraging advanced edge inference pathways to deliver real-time results directly to your device without long loading screens.

38 Disease Classes

Covers the most common diseases affecting Apple, Tomato, Corn, Grape, Potato and 9 more plant species. Our robust dataset ensures that even subtle variations in symptoms are recognized accurately, protecting your entire crop portfolio.

Transfer Learning AI

Built on MobileNetV2 pre-trained on 1.2M ImageNet images, fine-tuned on 87,000+ plant disease photographs. This advanced neural architecture understands deep visual features, allowing it to adapt to complex agricultural scenarios with unprecedented precision.

Treatment Recommendations

Don't just detect - act. Every diagnosis comes with specific, actionable treatment steps to save your crop. From organic cultural practices to targeted chemical interventions, we provide the localized knowledge needed to halt infections in their tracks.

Confidence Scoring

See exactly how confident the AI is with visual probability bars for the top 3 possible diagnoses. Transparency is key to building trust, so we provide the raw data probabilities, empowering agronomists to make informed decisions.

Works Anywhere

Web-based platform works on any device. No app download needed. Just a browser and a photo. Whether you're deep in the field with poor connection or at the greenhouse, our web design ensures you have access to expert pathology anywhere.
